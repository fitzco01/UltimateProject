Pick Up Where We Left Off

We built this to make keeping statistics in Ultimate easier. At the professional level there are people who are hired to take stats. If your team is like ours (ie. a college team without a coach) then the people who play are also the people who keep track of playing time, drops, scores, etc. Our vision is to have this application be an efficient and pain free way for us players to keep track of our own statistics.
